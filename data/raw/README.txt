This folder holds raw datasheets as .csv s that hold data pertaining to salal specific leaf area indexes and canopy cover measurements.

Data in the "raw_datasheet_03_13" is not a complete amalgamation of all the data collected for this project, but all of the data collected up until 2024-03-13, more data will be added as either additional datasheets or appended to this datasheet as collection goes on.
